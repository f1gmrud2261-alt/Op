About Continent

continent are huge landmasses on earth.A continent is one of Earth's seven major, distinct, and continuous landmasses, separated by oceans and identified by convention rather than strict scientific rules. From largest to smallest, they are Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, and Australia. These, often compared to giant, moving puzzle pieces, represent roughly 29% of Earth's surface and are defined by thick continental crust.
